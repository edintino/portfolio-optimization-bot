Evaluating Your Portfolio’s Performance

Backtesting lets us see how the portfolio might perform in a real-world setting using historical data, which we source from Yahoo Finance’s API. Here’s what you can learn from the backtesting results:

Summary Results

Each backtest provides a snapshot of your final portfolio value and total return, allowing you to see how your investments could grow.

Performance Comparison

Compare the optimized portfolio against a uniform portfolio to understand the advantages of a tailored strategy.

Investment Allocation

View a breakdown of your capital across different assets, helping you understand where your money is going.

Visual Charts

Interactive graphs display portfolio value changes over time, letting you spot trends and assess stability.
