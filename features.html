Features

Genetic Algorithm Optimization

The bot uses a genetic algorithm, inspired by natural selection, to find the optimal mix of assets in your portfolio. This approach selects combinations that best meet your chosen performance metric, such as maximizing returns or minimizing risk.

Backtesting with Historical Data

By simulating your investment strategy using historical market data, the bot provides insights into how your portfolio might have performed in the past.

Customizable Parameters

Adjust various settings to match your investment goals and risk tolerance:

  • Optimization Frequency
  • Transaction Costs
  • Top N Assets
  • Simulation Period
  • Historical Data Window
  • Initial Investment Amount
  • Performance Metrics
