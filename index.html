Welcome to the Portfolio Optimization Bot

Investing in the stock market can be both exciting and daunting. With thousands of stocks to choose from, figuring out the best combination for your portfolio is a complex task. The Portfolio Optimization Bot simplifies this process by using sophisticated algorithms to find the optimal mix of stocks based on your preferences.

What is Portfolio Optimization?

Portfolio Optimization is the process of selecting the best distribution of assets (like stocks) to maximize returns while minimizing risk. It's like finding the perfect recipe where you balance ingredients to get the best taste.

In investing, this involves choosing how much money to allocate to each stock in your portfolio to achieve your financial goals, considering factors like expected returns and volatility (how much the stock price fluctuates).
