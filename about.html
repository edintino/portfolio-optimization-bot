About the Portfolio Optimization Bot

The Portfolio Optimization Bot is an automated tool that helps you optimize your investment portfolio using advanced algorithms, specifically genetic algorithms. It analyzes historical stock data to suggest the best allocation of your investment funds across different stocks.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly: Designed for both beginners and experienced investors.
  • Customizable Parameters: Tailor the optimization process to your preferences.
  • Comparative Analysis: Compares the optimized portfolio with a uniform portfolio (equal investment in each stock).
  • Real-Time Updates: Provides the latest recommendations based on current market data.
